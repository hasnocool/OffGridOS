# OffGridOS

Offline-first operating system and platform for resilient, self-sufficient communities.

OffGridOS combines dependable local computing, renewable-energy-aware services, knowledge preservation, communications, maps, and community infrastructure. It is designed to remain useful when internet access is limited and to scale from a single household or vehicle to a connected regional network.

## Product direction

- **OffGridOS Media Server (OMS)** is the first consumer product: an offline-capable digital hub for media, documentation, local AI assistance, maps, communications, and network services.
- **Ubuntu and Arch editions** provide stable and flexible deployment paths.
- The wider platform adds energy monitoring, local AI, community Atlas services, resilient communications, trusted locations, and incentives for participating landowners and businesses.

## Principles

Local ownership, offline operation, repairability, open standards, privacy by default, transparent incentives, gradual adoption, and safety-first engineering.

## Status

This repository is the official planning and product specification baseline. Implementation is intentionally staged: validate the Media Server, then expand into the broader platform.
